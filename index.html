Who We Are

Formed in 2011 in Crystal Lake, Dangerous JOEs has been playing McHenry County-area establishments and festivals ever since. Between the members of this four-piece band, they have played private parties, bars, clubs, outdoor summer festivals, weddings, corporate events, community events, and local fraternal organizations.

Playing music from the 1960s to today, we have been influenced by a lot of incredible music and musicians. Rock, alternative - even a bit of country - we're not your bog-standard cover band. We like playing songs that make people say, “I can't believe they're playing this. I love this song!”

So, come on out. We'll get the party started!
